ADF Beam — standalone

No extension needed. On a Confluence page, open /wiki/api/v2/pages/<id>?body-format=atlas_doc_format in the address bar (it uses your logged-in session), copy the JSON it shows, paste it below, and press Beam. Scan the animated QR with ADFReader. A bare ADF document (starting with {"type":"doc") works too.
